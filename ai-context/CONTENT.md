## Copy Principles Applied

1. **Clarity First**: Technical concept explained simply
2. **Benefit-Focused**: Every section answers "what's in it for me?"
3. **Action-Oriented**: Clear CTAs throughout
4. **Aspirational Tone**: Future-focused, innovative
5. **Accessible Language**: No jargon, easy to understand
6. **Sustainability Angle**: Appeals to eco-conscious users
7. **Gamification**: Competition and tracking elements
8. **FOMO**: Waitlist creates urgency

## Tone of Voice

- **Professional** but not corporate
- **Exciting** but not gimmicky
- **Innovative** but not overly technical
- **Friendly** and approachable
- **Confident** in the concept
